I'm interested, how does this work?

The Procedure

Write to us using the contact form or email. Include the key details about your potential project to allow us to get a general idea and get a form of contact. Between us we can arrange a quick call of 30 minutes or less to further build out our plan.

We will then get back to you with a quote and Contract of Work Agreed and you can decide if you want to proceed.

Depending on the nature of the work we agree on, we will complete the project and send back to you for one round of minor revisions, if any are required.

Why does it work this way?

Off the rack products are already complete and come exactly as they are seen without changes. The nature of bespoke products means you can ask for as much or as little as you want and so prices cannot be made to be so granular.

Although seemingly formal, short Contracts of Work Agreed help us make sure that we fully understand what you require, remove avoidable adjustments, and keep your bill as efficient as possible.

OK, but roughly what does it cost?

As an approximation, our work is charged at an average of £30/hour. But what does this mean? Quite simply, the more straight forward the project, the cheaper it will be. To help you estimate here are some example projects and approximate timescales:

  • Conversation about the ideal setup for you and potential purchases [< 1.5hrs]
  • Create simple resusable opening title and closing credits [< 2 hours]
  • Create bespoke graphics or animate existing logos [~ 2 to 3 hours]
